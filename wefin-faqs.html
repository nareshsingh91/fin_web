What is an EMI and how to calculate it?

The full form of EMI is equated monthly instalment. It is the amount of money that is paid back to the lender on a monthly basis. The EMI is always paid up to the bank or lender on a fixed date each month until the total amount due is paid up during the tenure. You can calculate your EMI for FREE on the wefin app and website EMI Calculator.
